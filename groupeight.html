Step 1: Research...

We began by exploring what vlogs are and why they are so popular. ⋆˙⟡

Our goal is to determine the needs of viewers regarding vlogging content, analyze trends of different vlog types, and compare their popularity. We also aim to see watch time, engagement, and video duration preferences.

Vlog is short for video log, it is a format wherein an individual records themselves and their life. Viewers connect to vloggers because they are ordinary people creating content about their life.

Vlogging is most commonly found in pre-recorded videos, however it can also be found in live streaming. The most popular type of vlog seen in live streaming are travel vlogs, wherein the streamer is at a certain area, perhaps a convention or just a sidewalk, experiencing life while sharing it live.

Step 2: Observing Viewer Behavior👥

We explored YouTube to find out what types of vlogs people love to watch, noting viewer engagement like comments, likes, and shares.

Method Used (Observation – Empathize Stage)

Platform: YouTube

Top 10 Vlogging Content Types:

  • Daily Vlogs
  • Travel Vlogs
  • Informative Vlogs
  • Tech Vlogs
  • Motivational Vlogs
  • Educational Vlogs
  • Reviews
  • Gaming Vlogs
  • Cooking or Food Vlogs
  • Fitness and Beauty Content

Viewer Engagement:

  • Views: Trending topics or popular personalities get hundreds of thousands to millions of views.🔥
  • Likes/Reactions: Funny, relatable, or inspiring videos receive more likes and reactions.❤️👍
  • Comments: Viewers express connection, ask questions, and share experiences.💬
  • Sharing: Highly entertaining and trendy vlogs are shared more on social media.🔗

Step 3: Discovering Viewer Needs📺

People watch vlogs for entertainment, curiosity, relaxation, personal connection, and authentic content. Viewer preferences vary by age:

Patterns Noticed:
  • Younger viewers prefer fun, trendy, and relatable for entertainment and social engagement
  • Older viewers or adults often watch lifestyle, travel, or educational vlogs for learning and inspiration.
  • Authenticity and relatability of the vlogger are key factors in keeping viewers interested
  • Most vlogs are 8–15 minutes long, and viewers tend to watch around 6–12 minutes on average of a single vlog.
  • Shorter vlogs (3–7 min) are popular for quick entertainment.

Our Conclusion🎯

Vlogging fulfills various viewer needs, such as entertainment, connection, inspiration, and learning. Relatability and authenticity make vloggers influential. Different types of vlogs attract different audiences, making vlogging a significant part of streaming content today.

People watch vlogs not only for entertainment but also for connection, inspiration, and learning. The relatability and authenticity of vloggers make their audience feel personally engaged. Different types of vlogs attract different audiences: younger viewers are drawn to humorous, trendy, or lifestyle content, while older viewers often prefer travel, educational, or motivational vlogs.
